This is a program that helps users compute bank interest rates and its probable projection.

Instructions:

  • Enter the amount of your starting money.
  • Enter periodic deposit amount if planning for monthly or yearly deposit. (Optional)
  • Enter bank's yearly interest rate and tax rate.
  • Select dates for when to start and end the computation.
  • Review the provided details and click calculate.

Note :

-Monthly deposits are applied at the last day of the month.

-Yearly deposits are applied at the last day of the year

About this program:

This program helps users to compute for their bank account's balance projection based on the provided amount, interests, and tax rates.

Simple and practical project that maybe useful to some, especially on saving money effeciently.
